Agenda

  • The guidance has already existed for a while, and the management taking actions, the situation is not getting better. Simply not enough (struggling) students, will go to their educational advisors or teachers for that.

    One of my main goals is to implement a workshop for students on how to be receptable for friends who are struggling, and then how to redirect them to professional help, alongside with holding more information sessions about the professional help available.

  • Last year, as Head of PR, I gathered a website team and managed to get a CDE website up and running, despite fighting privacy concerns, logistics and team management.

    The second part of this operation has already been put into play, and has been verified that it is both legally possible (with privacy concerns), and logistically possible to display absent teachers on the CDE website. There are two steps left before we in theory can display absent teachers on our website.

  • Last year, I helped map out a relax zone, as well as completely alone 3D modelled the interior space, including bean bags, couches, tables and even a small kitchen type of area.

    This has been approved by the management, and is certainly achievable and possible to implement in the near future already.

    I would also like to add more comfortable seating, such as couches/ sofas, in the preaus and other spaces around the school.

  • The gap, between the CDE and the students, is too large. A big goal of mine is encouraging and empowering the students, ultimately easing the process for everyone to get involved in school matters, and ie. start competitions, help organize events or fight for change within the school. The CDE shall simply act as a bridge between the students & management, and help with technicalities if necessary.

    Not only does this give a fair chance to everyone to help if they want to, but it increases the (unfortunately none existent) school spirit. A big priority of mine is to make sure all years feel equally included, which means i want to ensure the younger years get the inclusion they have been lacking.

  • The entrance/exit queues are quite ridiculous, and ought to be reduced. I believe this is certainly something that is achievable, even within a reasonably short period of time. There are several options, which are all viable, and will be put into action as soon as possible after the elections.

    I’m confident the queue time will be able to be decreased and the process eased.

  • In making the school a more comfortable environment, doing what you love is essential, that includes sports too. Especially if it can get slightly challenging or competitive. That’s why I want to make sure competitions and tournaments are made, in various categories such as sports, arts, writing, gaming and debating. Here is also where I welcome all students to come to the CDE with your suggestions, help and feedback, so we can include everybody’s interests and passion.

This is a shortened summary of some of my agenda points for this year. Obviously these are not the only agenda points, however, just a few of them. If you would like the CDE to work on anything in particular, or have any feedback/ questions, don't hesitate to reach out to me through Instagram, Email, or Teams!
